    About Me

    I am Hung, a researcher focused on using computer vision and machine learning to solve real-world problems. My current research
    revolves around multimodal AI, particularly in applying vision models to semantic communication systems. In 2024, I published a
    research paper as the first author, introducing a semantic communication system that employs an efficient security mechanism, and
    it received the Best Paper Award. Additionally, my work in the industry has further enhanced my technical expertise. The experiences
    I have gained from both academia and industry have provided me with a strong foundation on my journey to become a research scientist.
